04Environment & Community

The ground you
mine belongs to
someone. We remember that.

Responsible mining isn't a marketing line, it's a ledger that has to balance at the end of the project. Land rehabilitated, water returned clean, schools still running, workers still healthy.

Land & water stewardship

Progressive rehabilitation plans written into every mine design, not bolted on at closure. Acid drainage management, water balance modelling, tailings designed to international standards.

Supporting local schools

We fund classroom construction, teacher support and scholarships in the communities around the projects we work on. Focus on STEM and vocational training for local youth.

Local hiring & procurement

Local roles filled locally wherever possible. We advise clients on supplier development so the supply chain benefits the region, not just the shareholders.

Free, prior and informed consent

We will not advise projects that proceed without genuine community consent. We help clients design consultation processes that meet ICMM Performance Expectation 09.
